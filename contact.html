Frequently Asked Questions

I specialize in 2D & 3D animation, motion graphics, VFX compositing, logo and brand design, poster art, and video editing. I love cinematic and anime-inspired projects, but I'm versatile and adaptable to any visual style or creative direction you need.
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A logo design may take 3–7 days, while a full animated short film could take 4–12 weeks. I always provide a detailed timeline estimate during our initial consultation so you know exactly what to expect.
Pricing is project-based and depends on the scope, complexity, and timeline of your project. I offer transparent quotes with no hidden fees. Fill out the contact form with your project details, and I'll provide a custom quote within 24 hours.
Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide. My studio operates remotely, and I'm comfortable collaborating across time zones through video calls, project management tools, and clear communication channels. Distance is never a barrier to great creative work.
Most projects include 2–3 revision rounds to ensure you're completely satisfied with the result. I encourage open communication throughout the process, so we catch any adjustments early. Additional revisions beyond the agreed rounds are available at a reasonable hourly rate.
I deliver all final assets in your preferred formats. For animation: MP4, MOV, ProRes, GIF. For design: AI, PSD, PDF, PNG, SVG. For video: H.264, H.265, ProRes. You'll always receive full-resolution, ready-to-use files plus source files upon final payment.
